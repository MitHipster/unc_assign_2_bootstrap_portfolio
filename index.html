About Me

Currently, I have over 14 years experience as a Finance Manager and Analyst with a passion for technology. I have wanted to become a programmer since I first started using VBA and SQL in my spreadsheet and database projects. I am pursuing a career as a Full Stack Web Developer by studying at UNC Boot Camp and Treehouse in my spare time as a Stay-at-home Dad.

I have experience in the following industries: SaaS, satellite television, and personal insurance. My hobbies are coding (of course), reading, and listening to a wide variety of podcasts. My children are my main source of inspiration and motivation.

My goals are to master the process of building web sites on both the front- and back-end, continually expanding my knowledge, skills and abilities.

I would enjoy working for a web design firm helping clients create an impressive online presence or for a company with an in-house web development team. And, if I can obtain a developer position that benefits from the addition of my Finance background, that would be better still.
